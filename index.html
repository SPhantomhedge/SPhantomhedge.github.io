About Phantom Hedge UG(haftungsbeschränkt)

PhantomHedge UG is a private investment entity focused on energy and technology sectors relying on Quantitative strategies. We manage currently exclusively our own proprietary capital and invest within the mentioned sectors with a balanced risk management approach. Our origins along with Academic qualification are from Engineering industry equiping us with complicated quantitative and AI fundamentals that support us in developing our proprietary risk management algorithms.

Our Services

Energy Sector Focus

We believe in a balanced and sustainable approach to green energy which allows us not to limit ourselves to either only green nor fossil fuels. This provides us possibilities to go long on futuristic energy technologies while at the same time invest in traditional energy industry that is required to sustain energy demands.

Technology Sector Focus

We focus on industries developing advanced technologies which drive humanity further into the future. Industries and products are identified and analysed leveraging our qualification along with our long term experience from working within the sector.

Risk-Managed Approach

Our unique quantitative risk management framework continuous measures the trends and volatility in the market with a goal to minimise our overall portfolio beta.
