Why We Built Our Own

Most risk tools we found were reactive and oversimplified. As active investors, we needed infrastructure that anticipates, adapts, and integrates deeply into our decision loop. That’s why we built Phantom RiskOS and Trade Intelligence from scratch — engineered around volatility structure, correlation breakdowns, and behavioral stress patterns.
