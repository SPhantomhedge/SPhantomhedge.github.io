Where Existing Risk Systems Fall Short

Most tools we encountered were tuned to static assumptions, lagging signals, or oversimplified exposure models. As a fund managing its own capital, we needed systems that adapt under pressure, integrate new information in real-time, and proactively surface hidden vulnerabilities.

Phantom Hedge began building internally — not as a product, but as a necessity.

What We’re Building Internally

Phantom RiskOS

Our internal platform for stress testing, forward risk modeling, and dynamic portfolio diagnostics. Developed to navigate correlation breakdowns, volatility clustering, and behavioral inflections across asset classes.

Phantom Trade Intelligence

Execution layer designed to complement our internal strategy — surfacing trade entries, volatility-adjusted sizing, and context-aware risk bands based on near-term uncertainty.

Phantom AI Marketplace (Coming 2026)

While not yet public, we envision a shared ecosystem for vetted, plug-and-play AI models used by funds — where Phantom supports integration and oversight, not signal generation alone.

Built From Within. Grounded in Experience.

These systems were not built for resale — they were born from our own constraints, frustrations, and design goals as a fund seeking asymmetric insight under uncertainty.

As we refine them further, we believe others — particularly those navigating similarly high-dimensional environments — may find value in the same risk-first philosophy.
