About Phantom Hedge

Phantom Hedge was born from necessity — not theory. In managing our own proprietary capital, we encountered consistent blind spots in existing risk infrastructure. Static models, backward-looking assumptions, and brittle integration held us back. So we built the tools we needed from the ground up.

Our Origins

With roots in engineering, simulation, and AI architecture, our founding team brought a first-principles mindset to a field long dominated by convention. We believe financial infrastructure should adapt to structural change, not just explain it in hindsight.

Mission

To develop forward-compatible, AI-native risk and execution systems that give fund managers the insight edge they need to navigate an increasingly volatile world.

Vision

We envision a future where managers — regardless of size — operate with dynamic, real-time, behaviorally informed tools. Tools that don’t just show where risk is, but where it’s heading.

We started building for ourselves. We’re now ready to share what we’ve learned.
